Linkedin Learning Video downloader

Hello, like me, you like LinkedIn trainings but you would like to be able to watch them in your mountain summer cottage without network or electricity!

Well perfect open the intro video and click once on the linkedin learning Video Downloader button.

The plugin will fetch all the videos of each lesson and download them.


Do not touch this browser window again during the operation.

open the first training video:

Icon blue:

Nothing

Green icon:

number of videos found to download ex: 1/28

Green icon:

more than 10 videos found to download

Red icon:

number of remaining videos to download

you will find all the videos in the "Linkedin Learning" folder in your Download folder.
